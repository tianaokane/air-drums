THE KIT

🥁 Snare
Centre zone, either hand. Strike downward sharply.
🔔 Hi-Hat
Inner top zone, either hand. Fast and light.
💥 Crash
Outer top zone, either hand. Big sweeping strike.
🟤 Tom 1
Mid zone, either hand. Deeper tone.
🟤 Tom 2
Lower mid zone, either hand. Deeper still.
💢 Kick Drum
Nod your head sharply downward. The biggest hit.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Click Start — allow microphone, camera, and face tracking
  2. Position yourself so both hands are visible in the frame
  3. Strike downward sharply into a zone to hit that drum
  4. Harder strikes = louder hits — velocity controls volume
  5. Nod your head sharply downward to trigger the kick
  6. Watch the zones light up as you play
